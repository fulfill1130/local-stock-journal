# Disclaimer

This project is a local stock journal framework for record keeping, review, and personal workflow automation.

It is not:

- Financial advice.
- Investment advice.
- Tax advice.
- Legal advice.
- A brokerage system.
- A trading bot.
- An automated trading system.
- A market data redistribution service.

The framework must not place trades by default and should not be connected to brokerage order APIs without a separate, explicit user-owned extension.

Market data may be delayed, incomplete, incorrect, stale, unavailable, or subject to provider terms and rate limits. Do not use this project to redistribute market data unless you have the required rights from the data provider.

Calculations, imports, assistant outputs, and parser results should be reviewed by the user before making financial, tax, or legal decisions.

Broker statements, official brokerage records, tax documents, exchange filings, and direct provider records should be treated as the source of truth when there is a conflict.

Use at your own risk.
